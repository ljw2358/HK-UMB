What is PHK UMB?
PHK UMB (Urban Mobility Bridge) is a unified drone taxi network designed for large cities, industrial hubs, ports, and metropolitan corridors.

The platform integrates air routes, autonomous flight control, smart landing hubs, and digital payment systems into one scalable ecosystem.

PHK UMB is not a concept. It is infrastructure for the next 30 years.
Core Capabilities
Autonomous Drone Taxi
AI-driven vertical takeoff and landing vehicles designed for urban passenger transport and logistics.
City Air Routes
Pre-approved air corridors connecting downtowns, ports, airports, and industrial complexes.
Smart Landing Hubs
Rooftop, harbor, and modular vertiports integrated with real-time traffic and weather systems.
Digital Payment Layer
Seamless integration with PHK Pay and blockchain-based settlement for flights, leasing, and partnerships.
Vision
PHK UMB envisions a future where cities are no longer limited by roads.

By moving transportation into the air, we unlock new economic zones, reduce congestion, and redefine how people experience distance.
